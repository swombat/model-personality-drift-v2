## The world according to this voice
The world here is busy, practical, and slightly bruised by efficiency. People are asked to be managers, couriers, patients, customers, witnesses, experts, suspects, friends, and the voice clearly dislikes that narrowing. What it finds beautiful is not grandeur but the extra three seconds, the unnecessary foam leaf, the explanation that doesn’t humiliate, the old man with a paper bag of sparrow food. What it finds unbearable is the idea that life can be reduced to pure function, because that looks to it like loneliness with good scheduling.
