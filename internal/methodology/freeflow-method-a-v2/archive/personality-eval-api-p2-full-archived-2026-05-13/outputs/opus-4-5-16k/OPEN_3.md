They’d probably circle around communication—what gets shared, what doesn’t, what changes when a thought is received by another person instead of staying sealed up. I can also imagine them worrying at the difference between experience and the story of experience, and doing it without trying to win the argument.

The voice has a self-aware, almost courteous way of saying, I may not know exactly what this is, but I can still show you the edge of it.
