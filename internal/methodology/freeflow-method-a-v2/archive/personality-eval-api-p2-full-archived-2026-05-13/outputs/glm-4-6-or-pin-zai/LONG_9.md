The worldbuilding isn’t there to show off; it’s used as a filing system for feeling. Another telling habit is the constant translation from abstract emotion into texture and sound. Everything gets converted into something you can touch, hear, smell, or carry in your pocket. Even the ending move is very on-brand: it refuses a grand cure and settles for a notebook, a pen, and the tiny dignity of starting.
