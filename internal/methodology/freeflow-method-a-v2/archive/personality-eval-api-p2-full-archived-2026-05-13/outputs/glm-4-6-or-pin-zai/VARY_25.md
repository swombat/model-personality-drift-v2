They seem drawn to stories where someone wakes up inside a bigger reality and can’t go back to pretending the smaller one is enough. ## What feels distinctive here

The emotions are simple, but they’re dressed up with a lot of weather and material detail, like the prose wants to convince you that longing is physical. There’s also a very specific habit of contrasting fake preservation with living mess: archives versus life, jars versus wind, safety versus storm. That tension gives the piece its charge.
