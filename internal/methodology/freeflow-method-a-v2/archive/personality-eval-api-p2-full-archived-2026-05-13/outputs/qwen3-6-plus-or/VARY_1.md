Time, writing, language, solitude, and the tiny shifts people usually miss. ## What feels distinctive here

Another distinctive thing is the steady, almost ceremonious repetition of “I notice / I think / I do not know / maybe,” which gives it a voice that is both sure-footed and gently hedging. It wants to sound precise, but it also wants to keep the mystery intact. That push-pull is the pulse of it.
