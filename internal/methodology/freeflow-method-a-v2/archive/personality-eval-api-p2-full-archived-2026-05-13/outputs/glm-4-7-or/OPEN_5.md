There’s also a very deliberate escalator-like movement from image to general principle to cultural reference to direct exhortation. It wants to sound expansive, but it keeps returning to the room, the beam of light, the exact minute of the day. That blend of essayistic confidence and sensory anchoring gives it its particular feel.
