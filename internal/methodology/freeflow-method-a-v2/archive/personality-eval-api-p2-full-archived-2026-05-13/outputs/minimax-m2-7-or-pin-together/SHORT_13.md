Another distinctive thing is the clean, almost polished cadence of the sentences: lots of balanced phrasing, little cascades of image, very few rough edges. Even when it says the reverie is brief, the voice still treats that briefness as productive, like the mind is a greenhouse where passing weather can grow into a plan. It has a tidy faith that if you notice something beautifully enough, you can draft it into being.
