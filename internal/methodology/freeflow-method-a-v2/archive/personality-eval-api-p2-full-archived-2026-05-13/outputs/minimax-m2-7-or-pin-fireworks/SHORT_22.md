It’s not just poetic; it’s slightly over-assembled, like someone decorating every thought before sending it out. That gives it a particular clean, aspirational sheen: no rough edges, no real self-doubt, just a steady insistence that reflection, writing, and daily life all fit together neatly if you frame them the right way.
