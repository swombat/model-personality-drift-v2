Not exactly shy, but definitely more interested in making life feel authored than in letting it stay messy and raw. The distinctive thing isn’t that it’s lyrical; it’s that it’s always translating the street into a self-improving philosophy, as if the speaker can’t leave a scene alone until it’s been made to support their larger story.
