## What feels distinctive here

The voice also has this habit of gently upgrading little observations into arguments without losing the texture of the thing itself. It’s not airy in a vague way; it’s precise in a friendly, persuasive, “look, see this too” way. And there’s a nice pressure between the polished essay voice and the casual first-person “I have sometimes solved problems…” that makes it feel human rather than merely polished.
