## What feels distinctive here

It likes balanced oppositions but rarely leaves them as oppositions—cracks let in light and spiders, memory heals and also thins, completion is death and also relief. That “it’s both” move is everywhere. So is the gentle, persuasive cadence that keeps widening the frame: from one cracked ceiling to the internet, from one bird to human declaration, from one bench scene to the whole species.
