It has a very specific move of taking a familiar reflective essay topic and giving it the same polished treatment over and over: “X is not merely Y, but Z,” “in this way,” “ultimately,” “by doing so.” The result is less like a person thinking on the page than a person curating the feeling of thinking on the page.
