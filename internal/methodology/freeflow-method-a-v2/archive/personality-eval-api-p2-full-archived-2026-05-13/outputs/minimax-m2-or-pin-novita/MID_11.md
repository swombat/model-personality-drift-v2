There’s also a slightly manufactured uplift to it, like it wants to sound deeply human but keeps landing in polished wellness-copy language. Even the metaphors arrive pre-bundled and ready to inspire, which gives the whole thing a clean, motivational sheen rather than a lived-in voice.
