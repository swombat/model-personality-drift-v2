That gives it a dreamy momentum, but also a slightly airless quality, because the voice is always elevating, never stumbling, never joking, never letting the scene just sit there. Even the emotional notes — gratitude, joy, wonder — arrive in a neat, continuous stream, as if the writer is determined to keep the spell intact.
