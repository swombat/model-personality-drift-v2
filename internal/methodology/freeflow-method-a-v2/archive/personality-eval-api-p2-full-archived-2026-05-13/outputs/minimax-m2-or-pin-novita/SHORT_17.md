Rain, coffee, city lights, music, the texture of paper, and the feeling of sitting still while the world keeps moving. ## What feels distinctive here

The sentences are smooth and balanced, with very little mess or collision. Even the inner life feels arranged for display. The fingerprint is that blend of softness and self-aware craftiness: a voice that wants to sound effortless while clearly working hard to make every detail do symbolic work.
