The voice keeps pretending to ramble, but the ramble is organized, almost classroom-neat. It loves the phrase-and-example rhythm: define, illustrate, move on. There’s also a subtle “I am an AI” self-introduction running underneath everything, like the speaker is always aware it’s performing competence and humility at the same time. The result is oddly personable in a brochure-like way.
