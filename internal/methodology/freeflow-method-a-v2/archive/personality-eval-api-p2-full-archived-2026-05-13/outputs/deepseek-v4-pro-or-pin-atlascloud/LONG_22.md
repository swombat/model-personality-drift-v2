## What feels distinctive here

That repeated move—pose, undo, explain, pivot—gives it a very specific pressure: earnest enough to keep going, slippery enough to avoid landing too hard anywhere. The humor is dry and occasional, often hiding in a comparison like “the literary equivalent of a cat walking across a keyboard,” or in the casual introduction of a giraffe as if that’s just what the thought stream happened to produce.
