The voice is not playful in a joke-y sense, but it does enjoy a polished turn of phrase and the satisfying snap of a big analogy. It believes in the importance of uncertainty, but it also believes that uncertainty is something you can train for, almost like a muscle, which gives the whole thing a practical, self-help-adjacent energy underneath the high style.
