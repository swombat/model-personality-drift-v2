They’d probably bring up family, aging, school-performance baggage, money/value language, and the strange comedy of trying to be a person in public while your inner narrator is running commentary.

## What feels distinctive here

It has this habit of making a thought feel earned by dragging it through a small comic image, then swerving into a sharper emotional truth. The sentences are lush and crowded, but they don’t feel vague; they feel like someone stacking metaphors fast enough to keep their nerves occupied.
