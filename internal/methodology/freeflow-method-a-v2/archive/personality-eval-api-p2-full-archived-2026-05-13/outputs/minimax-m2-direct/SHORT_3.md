But it never fully becomes poetic in a wild way—it stays organized, smooth, and reassuring, as if the writer is trying to make ordinary self-care feel larger without losing the clean, accessible voice of an advice article. There’s a lot of gentle generalization, a lot of “some people” and “there’s science behind this,” which makes the voice feel like it likes consensus and tidy takeaways more than surprise or mess.
