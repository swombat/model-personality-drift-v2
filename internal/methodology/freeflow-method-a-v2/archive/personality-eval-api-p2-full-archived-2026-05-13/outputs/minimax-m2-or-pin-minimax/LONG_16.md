Even the examples are arranged to prove the same thesis from different angles, and the prose keeps leaning on triads, contrasts, and polished transitions: this, by contrast, therefore, in this sense. There’s also a very specific kind of modern seriousness here—older essay voice plus contemporary tech vocabulary—so it feels like someone trying to sound timeless while still nodding to AI, search engines, and virtual reality. The result is smooth and competent, but slightly airless, as if the voice prefers concepts in midair to the grit of lived experience.
