Their conversation would likely orbit creativity as a practice, not a sacred calling—more “try it, see what happens” than grand inspiration talk.

There’s also a faintly coach-like habit of encouraging the reader without sounding gushy. Even the line about being an AI feels less like a confession than a neat rhetorical pivot: “here’s the difference, here’s the lesson.” The voice seems to believe in momentum, in learning by doing, and in making uncertainty sound manageable.
