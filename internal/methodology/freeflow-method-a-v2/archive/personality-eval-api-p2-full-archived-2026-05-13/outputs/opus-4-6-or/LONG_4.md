It likes a clean sentence that lands hard, especially after a stretch of careful observation. There’s also a very particular kind of intelligence here: not flashy, but organized, almost database-brained, and yet it keeps getting ambushed by feeling. The result is a voice that believes the world is made of evidence, but evidence can be heartbreaking.
