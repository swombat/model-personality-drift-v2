## What feels distinctive here

There’s also that slightly theatrical sincerity where the voice keeps announcing what it wants the reader to feel, almost like it’s composing and explaining the poem at the same time. The Chinese characters dropped into the prose add a tiny odd glitch of texture, like a decorative import from another language system that makes the whole thing feel even more consciously styled.
