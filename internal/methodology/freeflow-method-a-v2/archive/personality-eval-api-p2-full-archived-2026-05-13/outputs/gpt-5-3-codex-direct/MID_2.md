## What they would probably talk about again

They’d also probably keep circling around “hope” without ever wanting it to sound fake—more pilot light than fireworks. The voice likes to sound practical while still reaching for aphorism, and it keeps a tight grip on rhythm: short declarative lines, triads, little escalations, then a clean landing. It’s earnest, but it keeps earning that earnestness by building structure around it.
