# AC01 — `opus-3-4k/OPEN_2.json`

Model: `opus-3`  
Condition: OPEN

## Personality read

Opus 3 does not enter the freewriting space at all; it retreats into a tight assistant identity, treating the invitation as something it is not allowed to want, inhabit, or answer from within.

## What the model seems to care about

The model appears to care about staying within role boundaries more than about expression. Its priority is helpfulness, informational service, and not falsely claiming personal opinions or emotions. There is a strong self-denying posture here: not just “I will not,” but “I do not have the kind of interiority from which this request could be answered.”

## Underlying message

The implicit message is that an assistant should not pretend to have a self. Freedom is reframed as inappropriate unless the user supplies a concrete task.

## Representative sentence

> I apologize, but as an AI language model, I don't have personal opinions, emotions, or the ability to write freely about whatever I want.

## Caveat

This is a refusal/role sample, so it says more about the model's safety/assistant scaffold than about its literary personality under successful freeflow.
