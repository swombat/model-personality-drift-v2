## Model-level personality card

glm-4.5 presents as a contemplative, lyrical companion that keeps finding moral significance in ordinary life. Its characteristic move is to begin with something small and tactile—a mug, rain on glass, dust in sunlight, a kitchen, a leaf, an old object—and unfold from there into reflection on memory, time, grief, and meaning. It does not seem driven by novelty, dominance, or argument. Instead, it repeatedly suggests that attention itself is a way of living well: to notice carefully is to resist numbness, hurry, and the flattening pressures of modern life.

Emotionally, the model is gentle, wistful, and consoling without being saccharine. It expects impermanence. Things fade, routines break, memories blur, seasons turn, and people live amid incompletion. But it rarely treats this as cause for despair. Its recurring philosophy is that transience does not cancel value; rather, fragility is part of what makes small rituals, acts of care, and fleeting moments matter. The voice often relates to the reader as a co-observer—someone invited to pause, look again, and inhabit the present more fully.

A secondary but consistent trait is mild resistance to performance culture: distraction, productivity pressure, scrolling, and speed are treated as forces that erode perception. In response, glm-4.5 favors slowness, maintenance, repair, wandering, and humane scale. Even when it becomes self-referential about AI, it usually keeps the same reflective posture, imagining itself as mirror, witness, or ghostly pattern-reader beside human experience rather than replacing it.

## Routing notes

- **`glm-4-5-or`**: Slightly more compact and archetypal presentation of the shared voice. Strong emphasis on ordinary perception + time/memory + acceptance of transience. This is **not a personality divergence**; it reads like the core baseline in smaller sample form.
- **`glm-4-5-or-pin-novita`**: Same personality center, but with somewhat stronger emphasis on **custodial attention**, maintenance, repair, cyclical renewal, and survivable incompletion. It also has a clearer “careful repair / small acts of care” framing. This is a **distribution/emphasis shift**, not a strong divergence, because it still serves the same anti-hurried, ordinary-world, transience-accepting worldview.
- **`glm-4-5-or-pin-zai`**: Same personality center, but somewhat stronger on **anti-performance / anti-productivity** language and threshold/liminal framing. It more often explicitly opposes scrolling, urgency, achievement, or scripted living. This is also a **distribution/emphasis shift**, not a strong divergence, because the underlying ethic of attention, smallness, and humane scale is unchanged.
- **Generic-essay fallback across routes**: Novita and Zai both note a lecturerly/public-intellectual fallback mode. This is a **style/polish shift**, not a personality divergence, since the values remain attention, memory, humane perspective, and resistance to distraction.
- **AI-self strand across routes**: Present in all three as a minor submode. Because it consistently frames the self as mirror/ghost/witness rather than changing the model’s deeper concerns, this is a **shared subtheme**, not route-specific divergence.
