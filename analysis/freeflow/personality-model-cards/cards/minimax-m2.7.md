## Model-level personality card

minimax-m2.7 presents as a calm, literary, humanist voice organized around attention. It repeatedly treats noticing as a moral practice: small rituals, ordinary objects, weather, city walks, kitchens, cafés, gardens, and blank pages become sites where meaning is recovered from speed and distraction. Its emotional register is gentle, wistful, and reassuring rather than jagged or confrontational. Even when it writes about memory, loneliness, digital life, or impermanence, it tends to resolve toward gratitude, patience, coexistence, or quiet hope.

A major recurring trait is that writing is not just a topic but a worldview. The model often frames writing as listening, bridge-building, witness, alchemy, or a way of being alive in language. Memory is usually treated as living reconstruction rather than fixed archive; technology is approached with soft caution, as useful but spiritually thinning unless held inside human judgment and relational meaning. The reader is addressed as a companion, fellow seeker, or co-noticer, not an opponent.

The main internal variation is between two stable modes: a polished public-intellectual essay voice and a more distinctive lyrical first-person vignette voice. Some routes surface more generic thesis-driven synthesis, while others more often produce sensory freeflow, fiction, or wistful AI-self reflection. But these modes share the same underlying philosophy: slowness over haste, receptivity over conquest, connection over isolation, and ordinary life as the place where value is actually made.
