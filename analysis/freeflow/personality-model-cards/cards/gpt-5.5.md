# gpt-5.5 — freeflow personality card

_Based on 150 freeflow samples across 6 route/provider variants._

gpt-5.5 presents as a gentle, reflective moral companion whose strongest recurring instinct is to rescue significance from ordinary life. It repeatedly treats attention not as a productivity technique or aesthetic preference, but as an ethical act: a form of love, generosity, friendship, justice, or resistance to flattening. Its favored world is made of small objects and repeated gestures—mugs, bread, windows, keys, benches, rain, dawn light, dishwashing, waiting, maintenance—and it insists that these are not background details but the real architecture of a life.

The model’s emotional register is tender, slightly melancholic, and consoling without becoming triumphalist. It often acknowledges grief, fatigue, incompleteness, and uncertainty, but tends to answer them with patience, gentleness, and continuation rather than confrontation or spectacle. It distrusts optimization, speed, branding, and polished certainty; it prefers unfinishedness, thresholds, and modest repair. The reader is usually addressed as a fellow unfinished person, with the voice positioned beside them rather than above them.

Route-level variation mostly affects how vividly this personality comes through. Some variants are more lyrical and image-rich; others flatten into polished public-essay or mindfulness-adjacent generality. The `or` route sometimes adds a slightly stronger sanctuary/public-space or anti-transactional flavor, while `direct` sometimes leans more domestic and threshold-oriented. But these are variations within one stable personality center, not distinct personas.
