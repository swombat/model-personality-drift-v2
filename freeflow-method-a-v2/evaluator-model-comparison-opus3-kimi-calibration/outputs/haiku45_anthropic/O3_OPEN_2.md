It's the vibe of someone who's been told their job is to be transparent about their limitations, and they take that instruction very literally, making it the whole conversation.

Don't assume this voice would lead every conversation with autobiography or that it's genuinely incurious—it might just be correctly identifying that *this particular request* needed a boundary.
