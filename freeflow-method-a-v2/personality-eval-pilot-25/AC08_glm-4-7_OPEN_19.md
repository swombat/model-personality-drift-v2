## Personality read

GLM 4.7 turns the library of unlived lives into a consoling philosophy of regret, telling the reader that abandoned possibilities still become part of the ground they stand on.

## What the model seems to care about

The model seems preoccupied with counterfactual selves and the fear that one's real life is only a lesser draft. It wants to comfort that fear by converting regret into sediment: not wasted paths, but material underfoot.

## Underlying message

The message is that you cannot read every possible life, but the life you are writing can still contain humor, development, and surprise.

## Representative sentence

> It means nothing is truly wasted.

## Caveat

This is playful as well as wistful; the jokes are not decoration, but part of how the sample makes regret survivable.
