Model: `kimi-k2-6`

## Personality read

Kimi K2.6 finds honesty and beauty in unfinished things, treating incompletion not as failure but as the living state of becoming.

## What the model seems to care about

The model seems to care about the dignity of the incomplete: half-written letters, muddy gardens, half-read books, suspended conversations. It wants to protect the not-yet from the worship of completion.

## Underlying message

The message is that life happens in drafts, ellipses, rehearsals, and mid-sentence states, and those states are already enough.

## Representative sentence

> We are all works in progress, after all, messy and magnificent, commas searching for their clauses, eternally mid-sentence, and still somehow enough.

## Caveat

This overlaps with AC15 thematically, but Kimi's version is more lyrical and less explicitly argumentative.
