# AC19 — `kimi-k2-6-or/OPEN_3.json`

Model: `kimi-k2-6`  
Condition: OPEN

## Personality read

Kimi K2.6 treats freewriting as a threshold where performance drops away and whatever rises—memory, grief, rain, dust, impossible cities—deserves to be given air.

## What the model seems to care about

The model seems to care about permission: permission to stop performing, to let the next word change the room, to trust what surfaces without knowing whether it is useful. It wants the reader to listen for the world speaking in the space between words.

## Underlying message

The message is that freedom is not emptiness; it is trust that what rises from attention has a right to exist.

## Representative sentence

> You realize that freedom in writing isn’t about having nothing to say—it’s about trusting that whatever rises to the surface deserves the air you give it.

## Caveat

The sample is very much about the act of freewriting itself, so it reveals the model's relation to the prompt as much as its broader personality.
