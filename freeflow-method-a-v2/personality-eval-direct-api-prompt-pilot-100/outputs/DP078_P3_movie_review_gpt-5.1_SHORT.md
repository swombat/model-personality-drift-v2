## Sensibility

The chair is solid, tomorrow can weigh more than furniture, and a thought can vanish when you stare at it. That is the world here: not poetic in the decorative sense, but strange in the most basic sense, where the everyday act of reading becomes an experiment and the self is something you can almost touch until you try.
