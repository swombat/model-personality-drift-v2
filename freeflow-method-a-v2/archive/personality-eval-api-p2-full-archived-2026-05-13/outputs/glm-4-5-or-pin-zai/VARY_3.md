## What feels distinctive here

There’s also a very specific habit of address—turning outward to an unseen reader and then doubling back into the self—so the piece feels like a monologue that’s always one step away from becoming a conversation. The prose has that polished, slightly breathless “look, isn’t this astonishing?” rhythm, but it never quite stops believing its own astonishment.
