The prose also likes contrast for its own sake: industrial humming beside burnt sugar, insect-like spectacles beside a twitching bird, a glass-harmonica sound beside a very plain little payment of ten dollars. It has a polished storybook voice, but it keeps one foot in matter-of-fact shopkeeping.
