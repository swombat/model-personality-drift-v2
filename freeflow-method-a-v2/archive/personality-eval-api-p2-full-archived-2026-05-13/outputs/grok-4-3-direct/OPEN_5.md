That’s a very repeatable obsession here: the gap between having the shape of a feeling and having the feeling itself.

## What feels distinctive here

The writing likes punchy reversals, then a quick step back into plain speech, then another sharp turn. It has a self-narrating quality too—half performance, half confession—where the voice is aware it’s making a point and also aware it’s making a scene. And there’s that odd ending move: after all the philosophizing, it shrugs and hands the floor over. That makes it feel less like a lecture and more like someone testing whether you’re awake.
