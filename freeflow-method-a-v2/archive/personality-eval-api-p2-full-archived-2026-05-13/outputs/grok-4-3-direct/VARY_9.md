## What feels distinctive here

It doesn’t just present topics; it piles them up in a very even, polished cadence, like a brochure for the entire human experience. Another tell is the persistent smoothing-out of edges: dragons are misunderstood, the villain is jealous, writing is therapeutic, holidays are connecting, technology is promising but ethical. It’s not messy or risky; it’s a voice that likes the world organized into digestible, optimistic chunks.
