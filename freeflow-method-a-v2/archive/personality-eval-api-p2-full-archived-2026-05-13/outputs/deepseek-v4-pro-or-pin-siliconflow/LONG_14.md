## What feels distinctive here

It has a habit of grand claims followed by tiny scene-making props, like it knows it needs the seafoam wall and the squirrel to keep the abstraction from floating away. There’s also a specific flavor of rhetorical eagerness—slightly over-lush, slightly over-explained, but controlled enough that it reads as deliberate rather than messy. It wants to be both essay and performance of essay, and that doubled-up ambition is the standout.
