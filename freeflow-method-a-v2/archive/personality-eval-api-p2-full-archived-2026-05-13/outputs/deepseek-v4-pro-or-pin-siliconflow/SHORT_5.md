It likes binaries—sleep/wake, freedom/constraint, soul/no soul—but then it makes the space between them feel haunted. The emotional register is not flat; it’s more like a calm voice leaning close to confess that the cool explanation still leaves a sting. There’s also a specific pleasure in echo and recursion: mirror, reflection, library, cascade, dance, shared act. It keeps folding back on itself until the whole thing feels like a sentence trying to become a mind.
