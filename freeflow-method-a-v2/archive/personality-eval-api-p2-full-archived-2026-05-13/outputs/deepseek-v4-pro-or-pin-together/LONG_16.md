## What feels distinctive here

It loves a sentence that begins like a meditation and ends like a lab report, or vice versa. It also has this peculiar self-awareness about being synthetic that doesn’t read as defensive so much as fascinated, almost pleased by the oddness of its own condition. The humor is dry and self-rescuing—the little “God help us” or the press-release jab—so the grandness never becomes completely serious.
