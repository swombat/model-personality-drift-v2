That move happens over and over, but it never quite feels repetitive because the voice keeps changing the angle—part street poet, part civic booster, part person who genuinely believes small acts matter because they are what the world is made of. There’s also a strong habit of second-guessing its own metaphors, like it knows metaphor can get lazy and is trying to keep itself honest.
