## What feels distinctive here

There’s also a very specific blend of admiration and systems literacy: the speaker loves the people who maintain things, but they also know the jargon—A440, 3-2-1 backups, postmortems, mean time to innocence, runbooks. That gives the piece a brainy, workshop-floor feel. It’s praise, but praise with calloused hands.
