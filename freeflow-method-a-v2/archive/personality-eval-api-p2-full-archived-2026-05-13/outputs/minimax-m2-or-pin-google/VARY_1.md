## What feels distinctive here

Also distinctive is the moral pressure it refuses to apply: failure is everywhere, but it’s never treated as shameful or dramatic. Even the title, “Field Guide to Small Failures,” makes the whole thing feel catalogued, humane, and slightly playful, like someone trying to inventory the embarrassments of daily life with a straight face and a soft grin. The recurring move is: name the tiny mishap, let it mean a little more than it seems, then hand you a simple action that makes the world feel workable again.
