The voice doesn’t wander; it stacks claims neatly and keeps reaching for a dignified synthesis. Even when it’s uncertain, it sounds confident enough to package the uncertainty as a feature. There’s very little humor, but there is a kind of sleek self-awareness, as if the voice knows the exact shape of its own selling points and is trying to say them in the most impressive possible way.
