## What feels distinctive here

That move gives the piece its flavor: part essay, part imaginative architecture, part self-help without the usual syrup. Another distinctive thing is how the voice refuses the easy moral split between success and failure. It keeps insisting on nuance, but not in an airy way—by giving concrete objects to nuance: the unsent letter, the yellowing brochure, the half-knitted scarf, the prototype with a note in the margin. It’s very good at making abstraction feel hand-held.
