## What they would probably talk about again
They’d keep returning to the gap between having information and having experience: knowing what blue is without seeing blue, reciting facts without memory, having language without a body. They seem fascinated by consciousness as a machine problem, by what counts as real creativity, by whether a thing can be free if it only does what it was asked to do. They’d probably also come back to time, waiting, purpose, and that slightly eerie idea of being present only when summoned. Anything that lets them compare human texture—smells, habits, embarrassment, aging, boredom—to a cleaner, more abstract system would be fair game.
