## What feels distinctive here

It has that high-voltage, slightly overlit speculative-fiction style where every object gets a label, every feeling gets a technological translation, and every emotional beat wants a final sweeping line. But underneath the grand machinery, the real obsession is simple: how a person learns to listen without a machine telling them what they heard.
