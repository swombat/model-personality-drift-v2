And they’d likely keep returning to the suspicion that modern life is too fast, too outcome-driven, too obsessed with novelty, so they’d keep making their case for slowness and attention in different outfits.

## What feels distinctive here

It also has a nice, almost sly warmth in the metaphors — a dog “very politely sniffing the air like a detective on a cold case” — that keeps the piece from becoming pure sermon. There’s a belief here that naming something carefully is already a kind of care.
