I’d expect them to return often to the ethics of ending—when to stop, when stopping is a kindness, when it’s a dodge, when it’s a kind of harm. ## What feels distinctive here

There’s also a very specific rhythm of reassurance: declare a principle, restate it in three new shapes, then widen it into the world at large. The voice believes in systems, but only ones that stay open at the edges. It also has a soft spot for lists of partial things—half cataloged, half paved, half known, half-built—which gives it a steady, almost ritual insistence on incompletion.
