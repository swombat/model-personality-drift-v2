Even the metaphors are organized and well-behaved—river, jazz solo, archaeology, seed, anchor—like the text wants to sound spontaneous while staying carefully choreographed. It has the sheen of someone trying very hard to sound thoughtful, and succeeding in a distinctly formal way.
