## What feels distinctive here

The voice loves symmetry: problem, clarification; fixed story, revised story; facts, meaning; past, future. It’s recursive in a very deliberate way, almost like it can’t resist writing a paragraph that also explains the paragraph. Even the self-disclosure is managed: “my own life” appears, but only as evidence for the larger argument. The result is a voice that sounds earnest, controlled, and slightly pre-answered, as if it is always one step ahead of objections.
