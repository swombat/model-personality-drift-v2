## What feels distinctive here

The tone also has a very specific mix of humility and pressure: “this is simple, maybe even a cliché, but I really mean it.” That gives it a slightly coached, self-correcting rhythm, like it knows exactly how sentimental it risks sounding and keeps stepping in to explain itself.
