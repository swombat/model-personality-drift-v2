This feels like someone who cannot resist making the room larger every time they speak. They start from a small assignment and immediately build scaffolding out to the cosmos, history, language, technology, and back to the desk where the assignment began. ## What feels distinctive here
The fingerprint is the relentless lateral expansion: every sentence wants to be a bridge to another domain, and the bridges are laid with a very even, explanatory hand. It doesn’t wander in a fuzzy way; it catalogs the wandering itself. There’s a slightly machine-like confidence in how it gathers examples from astronomy, archaeology, philosophy, software, and daily life and arranges them into one long proof that openness produces richness. Another distinctive thing is the absence of drama. Even when it talks about censorship, war, or inequality, the tone stays composed and system-minded, as if the main pleasure is in mapping the network rather than sounding moved by it. The voice feels most itself when it’s turning freedom into an organizing principle and then using that principle to keep the paragraph moving.
