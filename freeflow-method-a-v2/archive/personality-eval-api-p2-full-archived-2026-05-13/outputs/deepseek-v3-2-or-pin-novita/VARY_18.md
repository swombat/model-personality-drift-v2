The voice loves balance and opposition—sharp vs. slow, now vs. long, digital vs. analog, here vs. there—and it often resolves those oppositions into a gentle, almost sermon-like conclusion. Even when it’s trying to sound humble, it has a polished, essayistic sweep that knows exactly how to carry a reader forward.
