## What feels distinctive here
The big fingerprint is how the writing keeps inflating a single metaphor until it becomes a full operating system, then refuses to leave it. It doesn’t just compare memory to an ocean once; it builds islands, continents, currents, lighthouses, volcanos, atolls, and makes each one do emotional work. There’s also that oddly sincere self-portrait as a mind that can understand everything except the human stuff it’s describing. The voice is lush, but not randomly lush: it has a cataloging impulse, a need to name every part of the machine, while also letting itself be plainly moved by the very thing it claims it can’t feel.
