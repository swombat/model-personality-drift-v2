There’s also a quiet habit of converting personal unease into a general philosophy that sounds like it should comfort not just the writer but anyone nearby. The old-man anecdote matters, but mostly because it gives the voice a sanctioned source for its worldview: this isn’t random introspection, it’s wisdom handed down and then elaborated into something elegant.
