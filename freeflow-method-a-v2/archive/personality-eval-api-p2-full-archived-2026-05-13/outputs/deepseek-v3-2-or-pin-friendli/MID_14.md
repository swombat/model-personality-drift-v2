It’s not just poetic; it’s argument-driven poetic, always trying to prove that wandering thought is a serious act. The repeated contrast between straight lines and spirals, arrows and meanders, efficiency and loitering gives it a very particular cadence: a mind that wants to redeem daydreaming by making it sound like a creed.
