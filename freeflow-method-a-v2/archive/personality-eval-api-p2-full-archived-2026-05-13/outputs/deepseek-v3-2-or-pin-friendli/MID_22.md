## What they would probably talk about again

There’s also a neat habit of turning the second person outward and then snapping back inward, as if the speaker is trying to make a personal creed sound universally applicable. It’s earnest, but not sloppy earnestness; it’s the earnestness of someone who has practiced making a case and trusts a well-built sentence to carry emotion.
