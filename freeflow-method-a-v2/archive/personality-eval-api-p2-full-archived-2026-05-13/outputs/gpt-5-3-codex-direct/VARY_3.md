There’s also a habit of speaking in clean, declarative lines that then tilt into image and aphorism, as if the writer can’t resist turning every scene into a usable lesson. The whole thing has a soothed-but-restless energy: wanting comfort, but not wanting to lie about how incomplete comfort is.
