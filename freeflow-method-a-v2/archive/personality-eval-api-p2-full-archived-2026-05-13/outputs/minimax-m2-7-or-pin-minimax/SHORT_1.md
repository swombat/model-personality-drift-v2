## What feels distinctive here

It likes tidy oppositions—bird vs. person, instinct vs. anxiety, shadow vs. light, noise vs. gratitude—and it leans on those to create a sense of resolution. Even the emotional turn feels prepackaged in a nice way: observation leads to reflection leads to a small vow to do better. That gives it a very particular softness, but also a slightly rehearsed, self-encouraging tone.
