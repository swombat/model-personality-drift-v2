## What feels distinctive here

It doesn’t just like metaphors; it likes metaphors that are themselves about layering, revision, sediment, overwrite, palimpsest, stratigraphy. That recursive obsession gives the piece a very particular grain. The voice believes in continuity through change, but it’s a continuity made of scratches, stains, and partial erasures rather than smooth development. It also has a knack for making the grand feel domesticated: Archimedes, the genome, Rome, and the Wayback Machine all sit on the same workbench as a desk gouge and a tin recipe box.
