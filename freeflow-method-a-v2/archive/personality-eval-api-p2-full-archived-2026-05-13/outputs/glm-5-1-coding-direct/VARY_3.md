The story loves scale, but it gets there through calibration and troubleshooting. Even the sentimental object—the ring from his mother—gets converted into a tool, which says a lot about this voice: it likes emotion best when it has to earn its place through function. The sentences also have that steady, explanatory rhythm, like a person who believes the reader will follow if everything is laid out in careful sequence.
