The voice has a calm confidence that keeps circling back to uncertainty, which makes it feel both composed and slightly restless.

Storms especially feel like a favorite engine here: they let the voice do atmosphere, metaphor, and philosophy all at once. ## What feels distinctive here

There’s also a very specific habit of making the private feel architecturally large: house, room, window, cathedral, river, raft, labyrinth. That gives the voice a roomy, resonant feeling, but also a slightly scripted one, as if it arrives already knowing the shape of its own conclusion.
