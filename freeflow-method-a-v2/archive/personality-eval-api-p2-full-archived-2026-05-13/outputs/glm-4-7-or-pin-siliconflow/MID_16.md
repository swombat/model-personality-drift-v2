## What feels distinctive here

There’s also a strong habit of turning private cognition into cosmic theater: a keyboard becomes a bridge, writing becomes rebellion against entropy, silence becomes an architecture, the self becomes a hostage and a conduit. The result is not subtle, but it is controlled; the voice wants to sound expansive, lucid, and meaning-hungry, with almost no interest in jokes, mess, or digression for its own sake.
