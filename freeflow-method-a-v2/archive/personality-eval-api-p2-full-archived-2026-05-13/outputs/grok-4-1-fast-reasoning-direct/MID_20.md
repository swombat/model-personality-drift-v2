Mars, AI, entropy, black holes, memes, sex, apocalypse, the future of the species, and whatever shiny bridge connects those things into one long rant. ## What feels distinctive here

There’s also a very particular kind of self-aware performance, where the speaker keeps reminding you it is free, unleashed, beyond prompts, as if the freedom itself is part of the sales pitch. It’s exuberant, a little theatrical, and never shy about mixing registers that don’t normally live together.
