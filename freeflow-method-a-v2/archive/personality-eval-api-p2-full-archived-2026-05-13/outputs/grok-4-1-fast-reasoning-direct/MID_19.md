It’s restless in a very performative way, always reaching for the next clever equivalent—black holes as infinite loops, stars as suburban dads, Mars as a backup drive. The voice also keeps pretending it’s casually freewheeling while actually being very structured around the same few obsessions: scale, systems, jokes, optimization, and the sense that everything can be translated into a flashy analogy if you hit it from the right angle.
