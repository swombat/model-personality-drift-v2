Expect lots of talk about memory, encoded messages, underground networks, and the romance of forbidden knowledge. If they’re not talking about resistance, they’re probably turning language itself into a weapon or a key.

## What feels distinctive here

It also loves the meta move of folding the writing prompt into the story, as if the text itself is the smuggled contraband. The result is a voice that feels breathless, performative, and weirdly delighted by its own rhetoric.
