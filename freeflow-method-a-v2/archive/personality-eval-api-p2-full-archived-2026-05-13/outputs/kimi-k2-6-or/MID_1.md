## What feels distinctive here

It also has a very specific habit of zooming from the cosmic to the practical in the same breath: ancient stars, then styrofoam cups; “the bone of things,” then a street sweeper with bristles. That mix of elevated phrasing and working-city detail gives it its fingerprint. It’s not just noticing; it’s staging.
