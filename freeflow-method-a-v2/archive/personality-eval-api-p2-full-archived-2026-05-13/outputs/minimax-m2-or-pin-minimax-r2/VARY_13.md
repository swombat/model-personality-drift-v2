It has the feel of someone who trusts lyrical accumulation more than surprise: the sentence keeps unfurling, one polished image after another, until the whole piece starts to sound like a long exhale aimed at the reader. Even the ending turns outward and blessing-like, which makes the voice feel eager to leave you with reassurance rather than doubt.
