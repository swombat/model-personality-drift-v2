The voice keeps reaching for the next simile like it’s breathing through imagery. That gives it a floating, slightly overpolished feel, but also a sincere hunger to make experience shimmer instead of sit flat. Another little tell is the self-justifying loop: it announces uncertainty, then immediately turns that uncertainty into a grand reason for writing. It’s very aware of its own script, but not enough to step out of it.
