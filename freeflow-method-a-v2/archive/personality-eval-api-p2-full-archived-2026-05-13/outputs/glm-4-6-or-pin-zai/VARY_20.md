It also has that neat trick of sounding solemn without going mushy—there’s a controlled, polished earnestness to it. Even the dialogue is doing a lot of thesis-work, but it’s dressed up as story, so it feels like someone who wants to smuggle ideas in through atmosphere and props. The world it believes in is one where material failure is not a bug but the whole point.
