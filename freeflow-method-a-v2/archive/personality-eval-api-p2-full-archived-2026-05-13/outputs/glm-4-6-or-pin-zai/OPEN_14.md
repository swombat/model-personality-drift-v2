It loves balanced, rhythmic phrasing and tidy contrasts: milestones versus margins, noise versus silence, sharp gold versus bruised purple. There’s a composed, almost essay-like sweep to it, but it still wants the intimacy of one exact Tuesday afternoon. That blend makes it feel earnest, self-aware, and a little bit performative in a charming way.
