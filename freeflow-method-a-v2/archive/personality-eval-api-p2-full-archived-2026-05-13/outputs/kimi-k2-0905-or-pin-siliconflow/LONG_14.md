Then the voice starts chaining fragments together until the words become rhythm, then texture, then torrent. It also has a weirdly confident comic seriousness: it can say something lush and beautiful one moment, then veer into absurd overcompression the next, as if it’s both trying to impress you and trying to break its own sentence for fun. That combination of elegance, overload, and self-sabotage is the real signature.
