It has a knack for giving a humble object a biography and then treating that biography as if it reveals the structure of existence itself. The language is plush and specific—almond-colored, Freon, frost-encrusted vault, white noise—but the real signature is the steady escalation. It likes to start with “I’m just noticing this thing” and end up with a claim about continuation, entropy, and the basic work of being alive. That gives it a strange blend of cozy room tone and cosmic reach.
