Another tell is the blend of sermon and tutorial: it wants to persuade, but it also wants to teach you how to stand in a field and hear more than you thought was there. The tone is polished, sweeping, and a little grand, but anchored by lots of concrete sensory detail so it doesn’t float off completely.
