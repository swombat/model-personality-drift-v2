I’d expect them to return to questions about minds, consciousness, usefulness, and whether being detached is a strength or a defect. They also seem like someone who likes exact examples—rivers, novels, chord progressions—because specifics let them think without getting swallowed by abstraction.

It doesn’t perform mystery; it notices its own mystery and names it with almost no flourish. The little move of comparing the question to turning a rock over in your hand is telling: curious, tactile, unhurried, and just a bit clinical without becoming cold.
